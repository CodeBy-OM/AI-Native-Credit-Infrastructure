# Vitto Website Sitemap — Deliverable 1

## Strategic Overview
The Vitto website must communicate infrastructure credibility to technical decision-makers at Indian lending institutions. Every page has a specific audience segment, a primary message, and a single conversion objective. Secondary pages deepen conviction; primary pages drive demo bookings.

---

## Pages

### 1. Homepage `/`
**Strategic reason:** The entry point for all traffic — paid, organic, referral, and word-of-mouth. Must communicate Vitto's positioning within 8 seconds and route each audience segment to the depth they need.
**Primary audience:** CTO, CRO, Head of Credit, Digital Transformation Lead
**Conversion goal:** Book a Demo (primary) | Explore Platform (secondary)
**Key sections:** Hero positioning, Problem framing (4 cards), Solution overview (2-column), 6 AI modules, Business impact metrics, Social proof, CTA banner

---

### 2. AI-First Platform `/platform`
**Strategic reason:** The longest-staying page for technical evaluators. CTOs and credit technology leads will spend significant time here before initiating a demo conversation. Must demonstrate genuine depth.
**Primary audience:** CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Credit Technology
**Conversion goal:** Request Platform Demo
**Key sections:** Module-by-module deep dive (6 modules), Agentic AI SLM architecture, Technical specifications per module

---

### 3. Full-Stack Automation `/automation`
**Strategic reason:** Operations and digital transformation leads need to see that Vitto covers the entire lending lifecycle — not just underwriting. This page addresses the "do I still need my LOS?" objection.
**Primary audience:** Operations Head, COO, Digital Transformation Lead
**Conversion goal:** Contact Sales
**Key sections:** 5-layer architecture (29+ modules), Interactive layer navigator, Integration diagram

---

### 4. Collections Intelligence `/collections`
**Strategic reason:** Collections is the highest-urgency problem for most NBFCs and MFIs. This page targets the collections head directly with language and metrics specific to their function.
**Primary audience:** Head of Collections, CRO, CEO (NBFC/MFI)
**Conversion goal:** Request Collections Demo
**Key sections:** Propensity scoring explanation, Channel automation, Recovery metrics, Field collections app

---

### 5. Agentic AI `/agentic-ai`
**Strategic reason:** Agentic AI is Vitto's most differentiated capability. This page addresses the "why not just use ChatGPT?" objection head-on and explains the SLM + RAG architecture in terms a CTO will understand.
**Primary audience:** CTO, Head of Digital, Innovation Lead
**Conversion goal:** Book a Demo
**Key sections:** Generic LLM limitations, RAG + SLM architecture, Three agents (Borrower, Field, Underwriter)

---

### 6. API Infrastructure `/api-infrastructure`
**Strategic reason:** Engineering leads evaluating Vitto need to assess integration complexity. This page converts technical skeptics by demonstrating API maturity, documentation quality, and integration depth.
**Primary audience:** CTO, VP Engineering, Senior Engineers
**Conversion goal:** Get API Access (Sign Up) | Talk to Engineering
**Key sections:** API design principles, Core endpoint reference, Code examples, Sandbox access

---

### 7. About / Why Vitto `/about`
**Strategic reason:** Institutional buyers do background diligence on vendors. This page answers "who built this and why should we trust them?" with domain credibility signals, not generic startup copy.
**Primary audience:** CEO, CTO, CRO — validation stage of the buying process
**Conversion goal:** Book a Demo | Explore Platform
**Key sections:** Founding story, Positioning statement, "What we are / what we are not", Key metrics

---

### 8. Contact / Request Demo `/contact`
**Strategic reason:** The conversion endpoint for all demo requests. Must be frictionless and signal that a qualified conversation — not a sales call — is what follows.
**Primary audience:** Any decision-maker ready to engage
**Conversion goal:** Submit demo request form
**Key sections:** Role + institution qualifier form, Message field, Response time commitment

---

### 9. Self Sign-Up `/signup`
**Strategic reason:** Captures institutions ready to self-serve their onboarding journey. Three-step flow with OTP verification qualifies the lead before it reaches the sales team.
**Primary audience:** CTO, Technical Lead — early evaluation stage
**Conversion goal:** Complete 3-step registration
**Key sections:** Step 1 (OTP verification), Step 2 (org details), Step 3 (confirmation)
